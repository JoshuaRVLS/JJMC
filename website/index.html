Unified Mod & Plugin Manager

Forget manually dragging JAR files. JJMC integrates directly with top repositories to make managing your content effortless.

  • Modrinth Integration: Search and install mods & modpacks.
  • Spiget API: Direct access to thousands of Spigot plugins.
  • One-Click Management: Install, update, and uninstall with a single click.
  • Dependency Handling: Automatically resolves dependencies for Modrinth mods.
